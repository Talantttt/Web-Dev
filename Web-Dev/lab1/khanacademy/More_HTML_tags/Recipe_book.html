Meatball Slider Bombs

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 25 mins
Ingredients Quantity
though 2
meatball 24
marina sauce 2/3
tablespoons olive oil 3

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a mini muffin tin with cooking spray.

Step 2:Roll one can of dough out on a lightly floured surface into a rectangle slightly larger than the muffin tin and place the dough on top of the muffin tin. Lightly press dough into each muffin cup. (Alternatively, place a second mini muffin pan on top of dough to easily press dough into each cup.)

Step 3:Place a meatball into each muffin cup and top evenly with sauce and mozzarella cheese.

Passion Fruit Mojito

  • Time: 5 mins
  • Serves: 5 mins
Ingredients Quantity
lime juice 1
mint leave 8
ice cube 2
club soda 2

Step 1:In a cocktail shaker, combine lime juice, simple syrup and 6 mint leaves. Use a muddler to crush the leaves for about 10 seconds. Add rum and 1 cup ice. Seal shaker and shake vigorously until outside is frosted, 10 to 15 seconds.

Step 2:Strain mojito into a glass. Add passion fruit pulp and club soda and gently stir. Fill glass with crushed ice and garnish with remaining mint leaves.
